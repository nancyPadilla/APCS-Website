This is my older sister's dog. Her name is Bambi but she doesn't know that because we always call her chispa, which is spanish for spark. She looks cute, but she is very agressive. She is a very soft dog and enjoys eating fruit and meat, but not veggies. She like being pet but not having air in her face.
