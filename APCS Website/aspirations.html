Sense of Accomplishment

Sence of Accomplishment is one of the 8 Conditions for student success that WHS has adopted. Sence f accomplishment is the feeling you get when you have worked so hard on a project, all on your own, and then you finally turn it in. It feels like a small weight has lifted off your shoulders and you can finally take a good nap. I feel pretty accomplished making this website on my own.
